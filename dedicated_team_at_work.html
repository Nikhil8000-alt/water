Dedicated Team at Work – Ensuring Quality at Every Step

At Aquivin – Powered by Prince Industries, our success is built on the strength, skill, and dedication of our team. Behind every bottle of pure drinking water is a committed group of professionals working together to maintain the highest standards of safety, cleanliness, and quality. From our production plant to our delivery network, every process is driven by people who care deeply about the product we deliver.

Located at Khasra No-1178, Bansur Road, Kotputli, our facility is not just a center of advanced machinery—it's a hub of hardworking individuals who inspect, test, pack, and ship every bottle with precision and care.

Meet the People Behind the Purity

Our team is made up of skilled technicians, quality control experts, machine operators, packaging staff, and logistics professionals. Each team member plays a crucial role in maintaining the smooth flow of operations and ensuring that our water is clean, safe, and consistently high in quality.

  • Quality Control Staff: Monitor each stage of purification and ensure lab-test reports meet standards.
  • Production Operators: Handle automated bottling, sealing, and packaging processes with attention to hygiene.
  • Logistics & Supply Team: Ensure timely distribution of stocked water bottles to our customers across Kotputli and nearby areas.

Why Our Team Makes a Difference

  • 1.Trained and Certified Professionals
    Our employees are trained in food safety, hygiene protocols, and modern water purification practices, ensuring our bottled water is always safe for consumption.
  • 2.Continuous Monitoring & Improvements
    We follow strict Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) and continuously refine them for improved efficiency and better product quality.
  • 3.Customer-Centric Approach
    Whether it’s managing urgent supply needs or solving queries, our staff puts customer satisfaction first—making Aquivin a trusted name in Kotputli.
  • 4.Safe and Hygienic Practices
    From wearing proper gear to regularly sanitizing equipment, our team follows strict hygiene protocols to prevent contamination and ensure product safety.

Driven by Purpose, Powered by People

At Aquivin, we believe that technology is only as effective as the people who operate it. Our team ensures that no shortcut is taken when it comes to product safety. Every bottle that leaves our Kotputli plant reflects the hard work and responsibility of the people behind it.

Conclusion

A strong, dedicated team is the foundation of our commitment to excellence. By combining human effort with advanced processes, Aquivin guarantees unmatched quality in every bottle of water we produce. Trust the people behind your water—trust Aquivin.
