About Us

Aquivin – Powered by Prince Industries

Aquivin – Powered by Prince Industries is a trusted name in the packaged drinking water industry, delivering safe, pure, and refreshing water to homes, businesses, and institutions. Established one year ago, our company has quickly earned a reputation for quality and reliability in Kotputli and surrounding areas.

Located at Khasra No-1178, Bansur Road, Bada Bass, Kotputli, Aquivin operates with a clear mission: to provide consistently clean and certified drinking water that meets the highest health and safety standards. Our fully equipped facility uses advanced purification technology and follows strict lab-tested quality control at every stage of production.

The company was founded by Mr. Vinod Singh, who also serves as the CEO. With his vision and leadership, Aquivin has grown steadily, focusing on customer satisfaction, product excellence, and eco-friendly practices. Under his guidance, we have built a dedicated team that works round the clock to ensure every bottle of Aquivin water reaches our customers fresh, pure, and on time.

Whether you're looking for reliable water bottle supply for your home, office, or commercial needs, Aquivin – Powered by Prince Industries is here to serve you with honesty, quality, and commitment.

Our Features

Aquivin Advantage – Features That Matter

Quality Check

Aquivin ensures excellence through rigorous quality checks, maintaining international hygiene standards, filtration integrity, and safe packaging — so every bottle delivers pure, healthy drinking water you can trust.

5 Steps Filtration

Our 5-step filtration includes sand filtration, carbon purification, reverse osmosis, UV sterilization, and ozonization — a comprehensive process ensuring crystal-clear, mineral-rich, and safe water in every drop.

Composition

Aquivin water is carefully balanced with essential minerals like calcium, magnesium, and potassium, providing not just hydration but also vital nutrients needed for a healthier and refreshing lifestyle.

Lab Control

Each production batch undergoes strict laboratory analysis, microbiological testing, and chemical verification under expert supervision — guaranteeing Aquivin's consistent purity, safety, and trust for every customer.
